Twivia Tutorial

Welcome to Twivia!

Twivia is a game that helps you get familiar with the tweets of famous people on the twitter platform. Twitter is used by over 330 million people that have varying views, poltical leanings, beliefs and so on. We all love to tweet and love to hear what our friends.


How to Play:

Select a category that determines the domain of tweets you'll be tasked to idenitfy the author from. For example, with athletes, expect to see tweets from active basketball twitter users like the clapback King Joel Embiid. Once the category is chosen, you'll be tasked with identifying authors of 10 different tweets from that category. Once youve answered all the 10 questions, you'll be ranked according to the following: 0-3: Poor, 4-7: Decent, 8-10: Great. Twivia is meant to be a fun game that helps you stay connected with the latest trends and how celebrities speak on social media so don't take Twivia too seriously, relax, learn, but most importantly, have fun and guess away!
